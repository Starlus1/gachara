INTRODUCTION

The goal of the project is to explore the psychology and behavior of high school students when playing gacha games. Based on the results of a recent survey, our team has realised the danger of this practice. To help sustain a healthy digital environment for students, we have developed GACHARA, a website that helps students better understand and become more aware of this type of game.

WHAT ARE GACHA GAMES?

Gacha games are a type of video game that has a random reward system to get characters, weapons, or pieces of gear. Powerful or rare items usually have a very low drop rate. To draw gacha, players can use in-game currency or real money. Scroll to see examples of rewards players might receive.

WHAT ARE GACHA GAMES?

Gacha games are a type of video game that features a "gacha" mechanic, inspired by Japanese capsule toy vending machines. In these games, players spend in-game currency or real money to receive a random virtual item with extremely low chance. Players can get these types of items as shown in the right side of your screen.

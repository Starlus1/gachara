INTRODUCTION

The goal of the project is to explore the psychology and behavior of high school students when playing gacha games. Based on the results of a recent survey, our team has realised the danger behind of this practice. To sustain a healthy digital environment for students, we have developed GACHARA, a website that helps students better understand and become more aware of this type of game.

WHAT ARE GACHA GAMES?

Gacha games are a type of video game that has a random reward system to get characters, weapons, or pieces of gear. Powerful or rare items usually have a very low drop rate. To draw gacha, players can use in-game currency or real money. Scroll to see examples of rewards players might receive.

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an expansive open-world action RPG developed by HoYoverse. Set in the visually stunning fantasy world of Teyvat, players take on the role of the Traveler, with a quest to reunite with their lost sibling while uncovering the secrets of elemental gods. With a rich storyline, diverse characters, and dynamic combat, Genshin Impact offers an immersive experience to players.

Honkai: Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail is a space fantasy RPG created by HoYoverse, taking players on a journey aboard the Astral Express. In a universe shaped by Aeons, players explore planets and uncover mysteries while assembling a team of unique characters with unique abilities. Combining sharp dialogue, cinematic storytelling, and turn-based combat, Star Rail gives players an unforgettable sci-fi adventure.

Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is a open-world action RPG developed by Kuro Games. Set in a post-apocalyptic world where humanity fights to reclaim its past, players take on the role of a mysterious warrior awakened from slumber called Rover. With fluid combat mechanics, parkour exploration, and a deep lore, Wuthering Waves blends action with emotional storytelling in a world teeming with both beauty and ruin.

WHY GACHA GAMES ARE SO ADDICTIVE

Gacha Mechanics

Sunk Cost Fallacy

"I've come too far to stop now."
"Throwing good money after bad."

FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

"Limited-time only!"
"I'll miss the limited-item if the event end..."

Emotions Hooks

"So close to getting my favorite character!"
"I will get that 5★ no matter what"

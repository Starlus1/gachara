WHAT ARE GACHA GAMES?

Gacha games are a type of video game that has a random reward system to get characters, weapons, or pieces of gear. Powerful or rare items usually have a very low drop rate. To draw gacha, players can use in-game currency or real money. Scroll to see examples of rewards players might receive.

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an expansive open-world action RPG developed by HoYoverse. Set in the visually stunning fantasy world of Teyvat, players take on the role of the Traveler, with a quest of reunite with their lost sibling while uncovering the secrets of elemental gods. Having a rich storyline, diverse characters, and dynamic combat, Genshin Impact offers an immersive experience to players.

Honkai: Star Rail

Honkai: Star Rail is a space fantasy RPG created by HoYoverse, taking players on a journey aboard the Astral Express. In a universe shaped by Aeons, players explore planets and uncover mysteries while assembling a team of unique characters with unique abilities. Combining sharp dialogue, cinematic storytelling, and turn-based combat, Star Rail gives players an unforgettable sci-fi adventure.

Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is a open-world action RPG developed by Kuro Games. Set in a post-apocalyptic world where humanity fights to reclaim its past, players take on the role of a mysterious warrior awakened from slumber called Rover. With fluid combat mechanics, parkour exploration, and a deep lore, Wuthering Waves blends action with emotional storytelling in a world teeming with both beauty and ruin.

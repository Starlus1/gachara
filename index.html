WHAT ARE GACHA GAMES?

Gacha games are a type of video game that has a random reward system to get characters, weapons, or pieces of gear. Powerful or rare items usually have a very low drop rate. To draw gacha, players can use in-game currency or real money. Scroll down to see examples of rewards players might receive.

WHY IS IT SO ADDICTIVE?

Variable Rewards

You never know what you'll get. The thrill of chance causes dopamine spikes, like gambling, which hooks the brain deeply.

Sunk Cost Fallacy

Players keep pulling because they've already invested time or money. Quitting feels like wasting everything already spent.

FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

Limited banners and exclusive units create urgency. Missing them triggers anxiety and encourages impulsive spending.

Emotional Attachment

Beautiful designs and stories make players fall in love with characters, making it emotionally hard to skip a banner.
